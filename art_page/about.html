Welcome to VI Photography!

I'm a Software Engineer by day, photographer by night and weekend. I'm currently living in New York, and I love everything about it, from the people, the food, rich cultures, to its history.

Personally, Photography is not only a hobby, but a passion. It allows me to explore the world around me through the camera lenses. Whenever I take pictures, it is not simply to explore the world, but I can find the ways to express the artistic side of me.

Being a photographer, I have a very different perspective on everything in life. To you, a photograph might just simply a picture. But through my eyes, a great photograph must capture the perfect moment in a fraction of time. For every picture that I take, there is a story behind it. My name is Tri Vi, and I am on the journey writing more stories like that.
